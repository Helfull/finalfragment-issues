# FinalFragment
## Master Your Collection. Scout the Markets.

FinalFragment is a specialized tool designed for the dedicated World of Warcraft collector. It bridges the gap between your personal collection data and the vast, cross-realm economy of Azeroth, helping you find the rarest items at the lowest possible prices.

Whether you are a mount farmer, a pet battler, or a completionist seeking every elusive transmog and recipe, FinalFragment streamlines the hunt by doing the scouting for you.

---

### Key Features

#### Precision Onboarding and Character Sync
Get started by connecting your Battle.net account to automatically identify your characters and their current collections.
* **Realm-Specific Monitoring**: Select the specific realms where you actively play or want to monitor the Auction House.
* **Cross-Region Support**: Filter and select realms across different regions to find items you are missing.

#### Automated Collection Tracking
Stop manually checking what you’re missing. FinalFragment automatically syncs your Warband’s collection data via secure OAuth.
* **Comprehensive Coverage**: Track a wide range of high-value categories including **Mounts, Battle Pets, Recipes, Armor (Transmog), and Weapons**.
* **Visual Progress**: View your collection status with "Missing" vs "Collected" labels and progress bars showing your total completion percentage.

#### Intelligent Interest Filtering
Focus on the items that actually matter to you by selecting specific interests during setup to eliminate unwanted noise.
* **High-Value Targeting**: Prioritize permanent collectibles like **Mounts and Pets** over transient items like gems or consumables.
* **Completionist Depth**: For those seeking total completion, optional filters allow you to track profession-based items such as Recipes or Toys.

#### Cross-Realm Auction House Scouting
FinalFragment monitors Auction House data across your selected realms to find the items you need.
* **Cheapest Missing Intelligence**: The dashboard highlights the lowest-priced items currently on the market that you do not yet own.
* **Detailed Listings**: View buyout prices, realm locations, and item IDs directly in your feed.

#### Real-Time Notifications
Deals on rare items can disappear quickly. Stay ahead of the competition with customizable alerts.
* **Multiple Channels**: Receive alerts via In-App notifications, with Email and Discord support currently in development.
* **Immediate Cadence**: Set notifications to "Immediate" to be alerted as soon as a deal matching your interests and budget is found.

---

### How it Works
1. **Connect**: Securely link your Battle.net account to sync your characters and collections.
2. **Configure**: Select your target realms and specific item interests, such as Mounts and Pets.
3. **Notify**: Choose your preferred notification settings to never miss a deal.
4. **Complete**: Scout the "Cheapest Missing" list, find the deal, and finish your collection.
